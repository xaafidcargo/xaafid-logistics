Customs & Documentation
How can I track my shipment in real-time?
You can track your shipment using the 12-digit tracking number provided in your confirmation email. Simply enter it on our dedicated Tracking page. Updates are pushed automatically every time your package reaches a sorting facility.
What is the estimated delivery time for international cargo?
Transit times depend on the mode of transport. Air freight typically takes 3-5 business days, while Sea freight can take 15-45 days depending on the origin and destination ports.
Are my items insured during transit?
Yes, Xaafid Cargo offers comprehensive cargo insurance. Basic liability coverage is included, but we recommend full-value protection for high-value goods.
How do I get a custom shipping quote?
You can get a free quote by visiting our "Get a Quote" page and filling out the shipment details (weight, dimensions, origin, destination). Our team will respond within 24 hours.
What documents are needed for international customs?
Typically, you need a Commercial Invoice, Packing List, and Bill of Lading (or Airway Bill). Depending on the goods, you may also need Certificates of Origin or specific permits. Our customs brokers can assist you with this.
